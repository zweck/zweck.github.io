The idea began about 12 months ago, I was in work, my iPhone on charge in the Apple dock and texting at the same time, I couldn't believe how frustrating it was trying to text and charge at the same time.
The steep angle meant that I had to twist my hand. I looked online for a dock with a shallower angle, and when I couldn't find one, I decided to design one myself.
My designs are heavily influenced by the 1950's and 60's modernism, using companies such as Vitsœ, Braun and Apple as benchmarks of good design. The 11° Dock presents your iPhone 4/4s/5 or 5s ready for use on your desk.
