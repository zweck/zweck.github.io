Hear from an expert

The FreeStyle Libre flash glucose monitoring system has been designed to be easy to use, which means that users can apply the sensor to the back of their arm themselves, without any help.

We have a series of short videos, each one focussed on a separate aspect of the FreeStyle Libre system. Click on ‘How it Works’ to see the list of videos available. We recommend you watch them in the order they are listed. There’s no need to watch them all at once, just choose the one(s) you need. If you need additional information, please check the user guide.

  • Teach yourself
  • Live online training sessions
  • In-Person group training sessions

We also offer various training events:

Teach yourself

  • Watch our videos to get a head start – click on ‘How it Works’ to find out more

Live online training sessions

  • Attend a session without leaving home by signing up for one of our live online training events.
  • Sessions are taking place regularly; review the schedule by entering your postcode below.
  • Information about the FreeStyle Libre system is presented by a healthcare professional (either a doctor or nurse) by entering your postcode below.

In-Person group training sessions

  • Meetings are taking place regularly; find out whether there is one happening near you soon by entering your postcode below.
  • Information about the FreeStyle Libre system is presented by a healthcare professional (either a doctor or nurse).
  • If you’ve already bought your FreeStyle Libre system don’t forget to take it along to the session – you can also purchase one at the event by entering your  postcode below.
