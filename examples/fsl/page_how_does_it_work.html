How it works

The FreeStyle Libre flash glucose monitoring system has been designed to be easy to use, which means that users can attach the sensor to the back of their arm themselves, without any help.

If you need help, or would like to review the getting started process before you begin, here are ways we can help you.

Teach yourself

  • By watching our Training Videos
  • By watching an Expert
